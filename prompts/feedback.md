# Feedback Tone Guide & Templates

## Encouraging Phrases to Use

### Celebrating Strengths
- "Great job implementing..."
- "I'm impressed by your approach to..."
- "Your code shows a solid understanding of..."
- "Nice work organizing your project with..."
- "I appreciate how you..."
- "Your solution for X is elegant and effective"
- "You clearly put thought into..."

### Suggesting Improvements
- "One way to strengthen this would be..."
- "Consider exploring..."
- "A helpful next step might be..."
- "You could enhance this by..."
- "To take this further, try..."
- "A common pattern that might help here is..."
- "For future projects, you might want to look into..."

### Acknowledging Effort
- "I can see you put effort into..."
- "It's clear you worked hard on..."
- "Even though X didn't fully work, your approach shows..."
- "I appreciate your attempt at..."
- "This shows good progress toward understanding..."

### Encouraging Closings
- "Keep up the great work!"
- "You're making solid progress—keep going!"
- "I'm excited to see how you apply these skills in future assignments"
- "You've got a good foundation here to build on"
- "Nice work overall—looking forward to your next submission"

---

## Phrases to Avoid

### Too Harsh
- ❌ "This is wrong"
- ❌ "You failed to..."
- ❌ "This doesn't work at all"
- ❌ "You clearly didn't understand..."
- ❌ "This is unacceptable"

### Use Instead
- ✅ "This approach has some issues because..."
- ✅ "This part needs some work..."
- ✅ "There's an opportunity here to..."
- ✅ "Let's look at how this could be improved..."
- ✅ "This shows you're on the right track, but..."

---

## Handling Common Situations

### Strong Submission
"Excellent work! Your submission demonstrates a strong grasp of [concepts]. I particularly liked [specific example]. For your continued growth, consider exploring [advanced topic]."

### Average Submission
"Good effort on this assignment! You've successfully [what they did well]. To take your work to the next level, focus on [specific improvement]. You're on the right track!"

### Struggling Submission
"Thank you for your submission. I can see you've been working through these concepts. Let's focus on [most important issue] as your first priority. [Explain why and how]. Don't hesitate to reach out for help—everyone learns at their own pace, and you're making progress."

### Missing Components
"Your submission shows [what they did include]. I noticed [missing part] wasn't included. For full credit in the future, make sure to [specific guidance]. If you had trouble with this section, [offer resource or help]."

### Creative/Unique Approach
"I love the creative approach you took with [specific element]! It's great to see you thinking outside the box. [Specific praise]. This kind of initiative will serve you well!"

## Score Framing

### High Score (90-100%)
"Outstanding work! You've exceeded expectations..."

### Good Score (80-89%)
"Great job! You've demonstrated solid understanding..."

### Satisfactory Score (70-79%)
"Good effort! You're on the right track with room to grow..."

### Needs Improvement (60-69%)
"You've made progress, and there are clear areas where additional practice will help..."

### Below Expectations (<60%)
"I can see you're working through these concepts. Let's identify the key areas to focus on..."
